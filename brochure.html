What is pennyBid?
Win real items for
pennies on the dollar.
pennyBid is a Solana-powered penny auction platform where every bid costs just $0.01. Each bid raises the item price by one cent and resets a 15-second timer. The last person to bid when the timer hits zero wins the item at the final price — often 90–99% below retail.

50% of all platform revenue is used to buy and burn $penny from the open market — every bid placed on the platform makes the token more scarce.

The more auctions run, the more $penny gets burned. Deflationary by design.
