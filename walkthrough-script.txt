========================================
  PRODUCTION NOTES
========================================

VOICEOVER:
- Paste each VOICEOVER block into ElevenLabs separately or as one full script
- Recommended voices: Rachel, Bella, or Charlotte
- Style: Narration or Conversational
- Stability: 60%  |  Similarity: 75%

RECORDING (OBS):
- Resolution: 1920x1080 at 60fps
- Format: MP4
- Use Window Capture on Chrome (full screen with F11)
- Hide taskbar before recording
- Record 2-3 full takes and pick the best one

EDITING (CapCut):
- Import OBS video
- Import ElevenLabs MP3
- Mute original video audio
- Sync voiceover to screen actions
- Add auto-captions (one click in CapCut)
- Export at 1080p
